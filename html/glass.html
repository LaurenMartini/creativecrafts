Hanzo Dragon Tattoo Glass Etching

A gift for my boo!

I etched this piece as part of a Christmas gift to my partner. They are a huge fan of Hanzo. I had an empty wine bottle and was randomly inspired to pick up my glass etching skills to recreate Hanzo's dragon tattoo around it.

First I masked the entire bottle with painter's tape. Next I marked the design on it with pencil.

Then carved the spots I wanted etched out using an exacto knife. I covered these exposed spots with etching cream, let it sit, and then washed it off.

With a base design etched into the glass, I used a dremel to add fine lines to really finalize the design.
